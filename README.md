# Project Sentinel

Project Sentinel is a comprehensive safety ecosystem designed for high-risk environments. It leverages a "Stealth-First" philosophy, disguising its powerful security features behind a mundane utility interface to ensure user safety without alerting potential aggressors.

---

## The Core Philosophy: Stealth & Utility

The most critical feature of Project Sentinel is its **Calculator Mask**. To any casual observer or unauthorized person handling the device, the application appears and functions as a standard, fully operational calculator.

* **Deceptive UI:** The app icon and initial landing page are indistinguishable from a native utility app.
* **The Secret Trigger:** The true safety dashboard is only revealed when a specific numerical sequence (the "Secret Key") is entered into the calculator.
* **Immediate Intervention:** Once triggered, the app transforms from a calculator into a high-utility safety hub.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional safety apps often fail because they are "visible." In a confrontation, an aggressor may monitor or confiscate a phone if they suspect the user is calling for help. Project Sentinel addresses this by:
1.  **Removing Visibility:** Preventing the escalation of violence by hiding the safety tool.
2.  **Bridging the Response Gap:** Integrating community-based support and AI-driven monitoring so help is never more than a tap (or voice command) away.

---

## Key Features

### 1. AI-Powered Monitoring & Evidence
* **Sentinel Voice Assistant:** Trigger smart actions (Emergency, Police, Hospital) using voice keywords even when the phone is not in hand.
* **AI Vision:** A dedicated "Capture Evidence" mode designed to record and log incidents securely.
* **9-Parameter Safety Audit:** Users can perform live audits of their current location based on lighting, crowd density, and other safety metrics.

### 2. Community & Trusted Circle
* **Trusted Circle:** Real-time status tracking of "Guardians" (e.g., Mom, Brother) showing their online status and battery levels.
* **Safe-Pals Nearby:** A community-driven feature that connects users with nearby verified "Safe-Pals" for group walks or immediate assistance.
* **Join Group Walk:** Coordinate with others in the area to ensure no one has to walk alone in poorly lit or unsafe zones.

### 3. Intelligent Navigation
* **Current Area Safety Score:** Provides a real-time safety rating (e.g., 8.5/10) of your current location (e.g., Bidhanpally, Kolkata).
* **Plan Your Route:** A map-integrated navigation tool that suggests the safest possible paths rather than just the fastest.

---

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React.js & Tailwind CSS
* **Backend:** Firebase (Real-time Database, Authentication, and Cloud Messaging)
* **AI/ML:** Integration for Voice Recognition and Image/Evidence Capture
* **Maps:** Mapbox/Google Maps API for safety-aware routing

---

## How to Use

1.  **The Mask:** Upon opening the app, use it as a normal calculator for daily tasks.
2.  **The Activation:** Enter your private passcode into the calculator interface.
3.  **The Dashboard:** * Use the **Map** tab to find safe routes.
    * Use the **Audit** tab to report local safety conditions.
    * Use the **Community** tab to find Safe-Pals or join a Group Walk.
    * Use the **Support** tab for one-tap access to National Emergency services (112).
